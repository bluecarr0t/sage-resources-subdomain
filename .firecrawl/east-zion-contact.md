## Our Location

East Zion Resort features two separate lodging locations. All [treetop cabins](https://www.eastzionresort.com/treetop-cabins), [yurts](https://www.eastzionresort.com/yurts), and [glamping tents](https://www.eastzionresort.com/glamping-tents) are located on The Hillside at 490 East State Street. [Tiny homes](https://www.eastzionresort.com/tiny-houses5-6) and [The Original Treehouse](https://www.eastzionresort.com/treehouse) are located at The Escape, just a few blocks away at 125 South Center Street.
