### The Forest Spa

Person sitting in a sauna, steam rising.

**Escape into the warmth of our wood-fired sauna, nestled in the Vermont woods beside our beautiful stream!**

Built from local and recycled materials, this cozy retreat invites you to embrace the ancient ritual of heat and cold therapy—nature’s way of melting stress away. Stoke the fire yourself or[opt for our pre-heat service](https://www.picktime.com/s/vEVqwW51CsPRKUJh4bxfK), so it's steamy and ready upon your arrival!

After a deep sweat, cool off like a true adventurer! In warmer months, take a refreshing dip in the nearby tub or the stream itself. In winter? Channel your inner Nordic spirit and roll in the snow!

We’ve got you covered with robes, towels, slippers, and all the fire supplies you need. Afterward, rinse off under the stars in our outdoor shower (weather permitting) or head inside for a warm rinse in our beautiful shower.

Available to community members and guests by appointment only, this sauna experience is the perfect way to connect and relax —Community members:[book your session and let the heat work its magic!](https://www.picktime.com/home/StoneCity)  Guests will get a special link after booking!
