### Canadian Room Comfort
Glamping tent: double bed, terrace with loungers, shared bath facilities access, electric heater.

### Canadian Room Family
Tent: double bed + extendable single futon; terrace; shared bathrooms.

### Canadian Room Group
Tent: 4 single beds (can join to double); terrace; shared bathrooms.

### Treehouse Tiny
Suspended treehouse: open double room + bunk room; private bath; kitchenette (stove, fridge); terrace.

### Treehouse Wide
Suspended treehouse: open double + twin room (twins can join); sofa bed; kitchenette (stove, fridge, microwave); terrace.

### Bungalow
Ground-level forest bungalow (same materials as treehouses): open double + bunk; private bath; kitchenette; pool view terrace.

### Sharing Villa
Shared hostel-style villa: 2 bunk rooms, shared lounge, shared kitchen, 2 shared showers; up to 12 guests; **2-night minimum** when booking one bed.

### Sharing Villa - Deluxe
Private wing attached to Sharing Villa: mezzanine double + bunk; private bath; kitchenette; A/C; more privacy for up to 4.

## Property amenities (from resort positioning)

Surf, wellness, restaurant/gastrobar, forest setting, pool (bungalow views), experiences program.
