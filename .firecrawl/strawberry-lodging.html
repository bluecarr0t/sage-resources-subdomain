Lodging

Reservations available 90-120 days in advance.

All check-ins are at 5pm and all checkouts are at 11am, if alternative arrangements are required please email info@strawberryhotsprings.com to request a time change.

What to Expect

Your lodging reservation includes full access to our naturally heated mineral pools. Overnight guests may begin enjoying the pools starting at 5:00 PM check-in and continuing until 11:00 AM checkout. For each full day of your stay, you’ll have access from sunrise to midnight.

Our accommodations are truly rustic—designed to offer a return to simplicity and connection with nature. Think of it as stepping back in time: no electricity in the cabins, and running water is available only in the shared bathhouses. There is no Wi-Fi anywhere on the property, encouraging a true digital detox.

Please note: Linens and blankets are not provided, so guests must bring their own bedding.

Strawberry Park Hot Springs 2016 PUD Amendment (PL-16-3)

Reservation Reminders

  • Reservation changes MUST be made 14 days in advance. — No changes are permitted within the 14 day limit. (No cancellations permitted)

  • Pool cleaning happens on Monday and Tuesday evenings AFTER HOURS.

  • Accomodations open 90 days in advance.

  • Single night reservations are opened 14-30 days in advance subject to availability.

  • NO PETS, NO RVS, NO FIRES AND NO PARTIES.

  • Driving on property is HEAVILY RESTRICTED

  • All accommodations share access to a central bathhouse with showers and flush toilets.

  • Small wagons or carts are recommended for tent sites and wagons to move your items to and from.

  • Groups exceeding 2 persons per lodging facility MUST seek permission to exceed our party size limitations. Email info@strawberryhotsprings to inquire.

  • Traction law in place November 1st – May 1st. 4X4 as well as snow tires or chains are mandatory for accessing our facility during the prescribed time period in all conditions. If your transportation does not meet our requirements, please contact one of our shuttle providers.

Winter Driving Requirements

From November 1 to May 1, Routt County law requires 4WD vehicles equipped with snow tires and/or chains to access the Hot Springs.

Dog Policy

For the safety and well-being of all animals, dogs are not allowed anywhere on the property. The parking area is exposed to extreme weather. Leaving dogs in vehicles is also strictly prohibited.
