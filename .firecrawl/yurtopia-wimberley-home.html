Texas Hill Country Glamping In Wimberley Or Dripping Springs

Experience a Secluded Glamping Retreat with Private Hot Tub, Fire Pit and Hill Country Views

While many glamping resorts cluster their units together, The Yurtopian Hill Country Resort sets itself apart with fully private yurts, each tucked into its own secluded section of the property. This sanctuary for rest, relaxation, and romance near Austin features 16 authentic Mongolian yurts across two Hill Country locations — 6 in Wimberley, 10 in Dripping Springs, offering five-star amenities, unmatched privacy, and an immersive nature experience.

It’s what truly defines the best glamping in Texas. 

A Sanctuary for Rest, Relaxation and Romance.

The Yurtopian offers premier glamping in Texas for or anyone looking to escape to a private, hill-country sanctuary. Designed for rest, romance, and reconnection, each authentic Mongolian yurt sits on a spacious deck just steps from a rooftop viewing deck with hammock, private hot tub, fire pit, kitchenette, outdoor shower, and private bathroom. 

Inside your climate-controlled yurt, find a luxury king-size bed, hand-painted decor, and a serene seating area, creating a beautiful, calming sanctuary.

Upgrade your experience with our on-site spa massage, room decorations with flowers or balloons, treat baskets, or a private catered picnic (Wimberley).

Whether you’re celebrating something special or simply craving stillness, The Yurtopian is the perfect escape—for couples, solo travelers, and anyone seeking a peaceful escape in nature.

Romantic Glamping For Couples Near Downtown Wimberley

Visit Wimberley

  • Hot Tub
  • Views
  • Stargazing
  • Near Town

Set on a wooded hillside as a private retreat, our six Wimberley yurts offer the most romantic glamping in Texas for couples. Each yurt features sweeping Hill Country views from private rooftop decks, a hot tub, a fire pit, and a luxury king-size bed. 

Just 5 miles from charming downtown Wimberley, you can explore local shops and galleries, dine at creekside restaurants, or cool off at Blue Hole—then return to your peaceful sanctuary.

Remote, Dog-Friendly Glamping Near Austin

Visit Dripping Springs

  • Hot Tub
  • Views
  • Stargazing
  • Dog Friendly
  • Extra guests

Our 46-acre, dog-friendly, family-friendly resort in the hills outside of  Dripping Springs has the exact same amenities as Wimberley; however, it offers a more options for groups and a larger territory for exploring. 

Hike to our seasonal creek, nap in a hammock or soak in your hot tub under endless stars. You’ll also find an authentic Hill Country lodge, ideal for small groups to gather before retreating to their own private yurts.  

New for 2026, we now provide options for groups and families: Three of our yurts up the hill, Annie Ger, Dottie Ger, and Amie Ger are set up with a king-size bed + two cots with bedding for either four adults or two children 5 and older. 

Located an hour from downtown Austin, 25 minutes from Dripping Springs and just 15 minutes from Pedernales State Park.
