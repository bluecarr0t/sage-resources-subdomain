About this Room

Lofted high into the tree-line, The West Camp has been meticulously designed to surprise and delight our guests. Your stay includes all the comforts of luxury linens, plush bedding, spa robes, and slippers. All complemented by an en-suite bathroom with a SunMar Composting Toilet and a safari-style sink. Stay cozy with the wood-burning stove (firewood provided) and enjoy morning tea and coffee on your private deck and sit around the fire pit as evening falls. While there are no cooking facilities, we offer a small but mighty room service menu featuring our famous Cheese + Charcuterie Sharing Board. There is a cooler on the deck with ice available on request. From May through October, refresh in our seasonal cedar outdoor shower. The camp operates on battery power with a thoughtfully designed off-grid water system, and we'll ensure your water is replenished as needed. Guests are welcome to bring their own food, order from our menu, or explore the wonderful local restaurants.
