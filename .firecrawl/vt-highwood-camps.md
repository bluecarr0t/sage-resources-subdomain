# Our Camps

Three hand-crafted safari tents, each with its own character and setting.

All offering the same extraordinary attention to detail.

![East Camp interior](https://highwoodretreat.com/DSC09184.jpg)

## East Camp

Nestled in nature and oozing with style, The East Camp's rugged elegance will transport you. Your stay includes all the comforts of luxury linens, plush bedding, spa robes, and slippers. Your private bathroom is steps away and uses a Separette Composting Toilet and safari-style sink. Stay cozy with the wood-burning stove (firewood provided) and enjoy morning tea and coffee on your private deck and sit around the fire pit as evening falls. While there are no cooking facilities inside the tent, there is a cooler on the deck with ice available on request. Guests are welcome to bring their own food and supplies to cook over the fire pit, or explore the wonderful local restaurants. From May through October, refresh in our seasonal cedar outdoor shower. The camp operates on battery power with a thoughtfully designed off-grid water system, and we'll ensure your water is replenished as needed.

Your stay includes all the comforts of luxury linens, plush bedding, spa robes, and slippers. All complemented by an en-suite bathroom with a SunMar Composting Toilet and a safari-style sink. Stay cozy with the wood-burning stove (firewood provided) and enjoy morning tea and coffee on your private deck and sit around the fire pit as evening falls. While there are no cooking facilities inside the tent, there is a cooler on the deck with ice available on request. Guests are welcome to bring their own food and supplies to cook over the fire pit, or explore the wonderful local restaurants. From May through October, refresh in our seasonal cedar outdoor shower.
