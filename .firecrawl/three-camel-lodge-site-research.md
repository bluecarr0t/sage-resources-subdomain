# Three Camel Lodge — site inventory research

**Property:** Three Camel Lodge (Nomadic Expeditions journeys)  
**Research date:** 2026-05-21

## Property summary

- **Location:** Gobi Altai Mountains, South Gobi, Mongolia (near Dalanzadgad; flights via Ulaanbaatar)  
- **Inventory:** **40 luxury ger tents** (handmade by local artisans) in three categories  
- **Operator / journeys:** Nomadic Expeditions land packages; Beyond Green member  
- **Phone:** 800-998-6634 | **Email:** info@threecamellodge.com  

## Ger categories (3 bookable types)

| Site name | Layout | Capacity | Notes |
|-----------|--------|----------|-------|
| Deluxe Ger | King or two twins; en-suite bathroom | 2 | Default on 2-night land packages |
| Chinggis Khan Ger | King bedroom, private bath, relaxation area, large living space | 2 | Premium space |
| Khubilai Khan Ger | Two bedrooms connected by bath; king + two twins in second room | 3–4 | Family ger |

## Shared ger amenities

Wool carpets; camel-hair blankets; hand-carved/painted Mongolian furniture; wood stove or electric heat; solar LED lighting; private stone en-suite bathroom.

## Lodge & experiences

Spa, Bulagtai Restaurant, Thirsty Camel Bar, stables, greenhouse, screening room, Dino Hall; excursions to Khongoryn Els (Singing Sands), Flaming Cliffs, petroglyphs, Yol Valley; horseback riding, stargazing, ger building, archery, and more.
