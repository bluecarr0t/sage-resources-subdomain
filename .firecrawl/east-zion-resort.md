## About the Resort

Welcome to East Zion Resort, a luxury glamping resort and destination near Zion National Park in Orderville, Utah. The resort boasts a diverse range of accommodations, including yurts, glamping tents, treehouses, treetop cabins, tiny homes, mirror houses, and stargazing container cabins. Surrounded by an array of natural wonders, East Zion Resort provides not just a place to rest your head but serves as the ultimate gateway to adventure in Southern Utah. Searching for unique and unforgettable places to stay near Zion National Park? East Zion Resort is a must-visit destination recognized by the top travel experts worldwide.
