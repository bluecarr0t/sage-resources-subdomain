**Brand:** Four Seasons  
**Research date:** 2026-05-21  
**Primary source:** https://www.fourseasons.com/goldentriangle/accommodations/

## Property summary

- **Location:** Golden Triangle, Chiang Rai, Thailand (Ruak River; Thailand / Laos / Myanmar confluence)
- **Access:** Longtail boat along Ruak River (per property marketing)
- **Inventory:** **15 exclusive luxury tents** + **one two-bedroom Explorer's Lodge** (16 accommodations per FS “By Design” copy)
- **Bookable categories on FS site:** 4 (3 tent types + 1 lodge)

- **Phone:** +66 (53) 910 200
