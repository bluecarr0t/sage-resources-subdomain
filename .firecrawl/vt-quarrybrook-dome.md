# An oasis away from the city.

Let us share our little piece of Vermont with you!

Miles of trails accessible from your site. Abandoned rock quarry for taking a dip or a short canoe paddle.

.

## What to Expect

Located off a dirt road, tucked into a corner of our working homestead, you'll find a space to relax, unwind, and reconnect.

The dome is fully furnished with a queen size bed and linens. The wood stove will keep you toasty on a chilly Vermont evening.

Cozy up next to the fire pit while soaking in the stars. Firewood provided.

The private picnic area includes a wash station with basic cooking gear (e.g., propane cooking stove, french press, plates, silverware, pan). We will provide potable water.

We have an outhouse with a dry sawdust composting bucket arrangement. Clear instructions will be provided upon your arrival.

The dome is insulated, and available for hardy souls looking for winter camping options, snowshoeing, skiing, etc.

Dogs welcome. We expect you'll clean-up after them in a reasonable manner. Please be mindful our chickens are free range. We also ask that you don’t leave them on the site unattended.

Trash must be left in designated receipticles.

Oh, and one last thing! If you have a 4-wheel drive vehicle, and would prefer to drive to the site, we can discuss.
