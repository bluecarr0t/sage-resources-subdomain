## Like nothing you've experienced before

Every element of Highwood Retreat has been crafted to delight and surprise—from exquisite linens and curated treasures to plush robes waiting by your bed and a meticulous playlist setting the mood.


This is not camping. This is an immersive escape—build your own fire by lantern light, roam our wild hillsides, and fall asleep to owls in the Vermont woods. All while feeling more elegant and decadent than you ever thought possible.
