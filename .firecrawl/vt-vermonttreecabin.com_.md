## What We Offer

Escape the chaos of daily life and embrace tranquility at the Vermont Tree Cabin property, where you’ll find the perfect blend of privacy and modern comfort.

Reconnect with nature and recharge your spirit in this cozy and idyllic sanctuary, far removed from the hustle and bustle of the everyday world.

### Waterfront

Beautiful waterfront views and private access to Walker Pond.

### Kitchen

Includes induction stove, toaster oven, coffee maker, refrigerator, and full sink.

### Firepit

Relax in Adirondack chairs by the firepit, savoring glistening waterfront views.

### Hot Tub

Handmade cedar hot tub available year round.

### WiFi

Free WiFi available, including access to Netflix and Amazon Prime Video.

### Bathroom

Even amidst treetops, enjoy a hot shower and modern bathroom luxuries.
