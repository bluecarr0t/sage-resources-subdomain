# What's Nearby

There's a million reasons we've chosen this area to call home. Spend your time just being, or explore the many farms, restaurants, galleries, shops, and activities Southern Vermont has to offer.

We’re located 5 miles from downtown Putney where you’ll find [Next Stage Arts](http://nextstagearts.org/), [Yellow Barn International Music Center](https://www.yellowbarn.org/), and the [Sandglass Theater](http://sandglasstheater.org/). We also have a great food [co-op](http://www.putneycoop.com/), general store, and a few restaurants.

If you’re looking for a hike with a great view, it’s absolutely worth checking out [P](https://www.putneymountain.org/web/) [utney Mountain](https://www.putneymountain.org/web/) and [The Pinnacle](http://www.windmillhillpinnacle.org/). Easily hiked in a couple of hours or less.

We’re 17 miles from Brattleboro - where you’ll find a larger variety of restaurants and local shops. We’re also blessed with a [year-round farmers market](https://www.brattleboroareafarmersmarket.com/) \- which is not to be missed!

We’re passionate about good quality food and supporting our local farmers and businesses. We would love the opportunity to recommend some of our favorite spots.
