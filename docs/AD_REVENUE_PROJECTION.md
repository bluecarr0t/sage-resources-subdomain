## Ad Network Recommendations

### Primary Networks

#### 1. Google AdSense
- **Pros:** Easy setup, reliable payments, broad advertiser base
- **Cons:** Lower RPMs, limited control
- **Best For:** Foundation phase, all page types
- **Expected RPM:** $3-8

#### 2. Media.net (Yahoo/Bing)
- **Pros:** Contextual targeting, competitive rates
- **Cons:** Smaller advertiser pool
- **Best For:** B2B content, professional services
- **Expected RPM:** $5-12

#### 3. Ezoic
- **Pros:** AI optimization, higher RPMs, easy integration
- **Cons:** Revenue share model
- **Best For:** Growth phase, optimization
- **Expected RPM:** $6-15

#### 4. AdThrive / Mediavine
- **Pros:** Premium rates, excellent support, high RPMs
- **Cons:** Traffic requirements (50K+ monthly sessions)
- **Best For:** Maturity phase (Month 7+)
- **Expected RPM:** $10-25

### Specialized Networks

#### 5. Outbrain / Taboola (Native)
- **Pros:** Native ad format, high engagement
- **Cons:** Lower RPMs, content quality concerns
- **Best For:** Guide pages, long-form content
- **Expected RPM:** $4-10

#### 6. Direct Advertisers
- **Pros:** Highest rates, full control, premium relationships
- **Cons:** Requires sales effort, relationship management
- **Best For:** B2B landing pages, high-traffic pages
- **Expected RPM:** $15-40
