## Content Template Guidelines

### Location-Based Pages
**Structure:**
- Hero: "[Service] in [State]" headline
- Section 1: "[State] [Service] Expertise"
- Section 2: "Why [State] is Ideal for [Property Type]"
- Benefits section (state-specific)
- CTA section
- Keywords: [service] [state], [state] [property type] [service]

**Word Count:** 800-1,200 words

**SEO Elements:**
- Location field in metadata
- State-specific keywords
- Local market insights
- Regional competitor mentions

---

### Problem/Solution Pages
**Structure:**
- Hero: Question or solution-focused headline
- Section 1: Problem definition
- Section 2: Solution overview
- Section 3: Step-by-step process (HowTo schema)
- FAQ section (FAQPage schema)
- CTA section

**Word Count:** 1,200-1,800 words

**SEO Elements:**
- Question-focused keywords
- HowTo structured data
- FAQ structured data
- Step-by-step format for featured snippets

---

### FAQ Pages
**Structure:**
- Hero: "[Topic] FAQ" headline
- Section: "Common Questions About [Topic]"
- 8-12 FAQ items with detailed answers
- Related services section
- CTA section

**Word Count:** 1,000-1,500 words

**SEO Elements:**
- FAQPage structured data
- Concise answers (40-60 words) for featured snippets
- Comprehensive coverage of common questions

---

### Comparison Pages
**Structure:**
- Hero: "[Option A] vs [Option B]" headline
- Comparison table or section
- When to choose Option A
- When to choose Option B
- Conclusion/recommendation
- FAQ section

**Word Count:** 1,500-2,000 words

**SEO Elements:**
- Comparison keywords
- Table format for featured snippets
- Side-by-side analysis

## SEO Best Practices for New Pages

### 1. Keyword Research
- Target primary keyword in H1 and title
- Include 3-5 secondary keywords naturally
- Use long-tail variations throughout

### 2. Internal Linking
- Link to 3-5 related landing pages
- Link to relevant glossary terms (2-3)
- Link to related pillar pages (1-2)
- Link to root domain service pages

### 3. Structured Data
- Service schema for service pages
- FAQPage schema for FAQ sections
- HowTo schema for process pages
- BreadcrumbList schema (automatic)
- LocalBusiness schema (automatic)

### 4. Content Quality
- Minimum 800 words (1,200+ preferred)
- Unique, valuable content (no duplication)
- Natural keyword usage
- Readable, scannable format

### 5. Meta Tags
- Title: 50-60 characters
- Description: 150-160 characters
- Include primary keyword in both
