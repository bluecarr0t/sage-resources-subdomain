This document provides a comprehensive analysis of the current glossary system and recommendations for expansion. ## Content Quality Standards

For each new term, ensure:

1. **Short Definition:** 1-2 sentences, optimized for featured snippets
2. **Extended Definition:** 300-500 words with context specific to outdoor hospitality
3. **Examples:** 2-3 real-world scenarios relevant to glamping/RV/campground properties
4. **Use Cases:** 2-3 practical applications
5. **Related Terms:** 3-5 internal links to other glossary terms
6. **SEO Keywords:** 5-10 relevant keywords
7. **Internal Links:** 3-5 links to landing pages and service pages
8. **FAQs:** 3-5 questions with detailed answers
9. **Schema Markup:** DefinedTerm and FAQPage schema (already implemented)
