## Current Unit Type Glossary Terms

1. **A-Frame** - Triangular structure with steeply angled sides

3. **Bell Tent** - Circular canvas tent with single central pole
4. **Cabin** - Small wooden house in natural setting
5. **Canvas Tent** - Heavy-duty canvas tent structure
6. **Covered Wagon** - Replica Conestoga wagon accommodation
7. **Dome** - Geodesic dome structure
8. **Safari Tent** - Large rectangular canvas tent with straight walls
9. **Tiny Home** - Compact dwelling under 400 sq ft
10. **Tipi** - Conical tent structure (Native American style)
11. **Treehouse** - Elevated structure built in trees
12. **Vintage Trailer** - Restored mid-20th century travel trailer
13. **Yurt** - Circular tent with wooden lattice frame

## Suggested Additional Unit Type Terms

### Priority 1: High-Value Terms (Commonly Used in Industry)

#### 1. **Glamping Pod** ⭐⭐⭐
**Rationale:**
- Frequently mentioned in industry discussions
- Distinct from other tent types (insulated, permanent structure)
- Growing market segment
- Referenced in codebase analysis scripts

**Definition:** A small, insulated, permanent or semi-permanent glamping accommodation structure, typically featuring a compact design with modern amenities, designed for year-round use in various climates.

**SEO Keywords:** glamping pod, glamping pod accommodation, insulated glamping pod, pod glamping

**Related Terms:** glamping, cabin, tiny-home, canvas-tent

---

#### 2. **Shepherd's Hut** ⭐⭐⭐
**Rationale:**
- Traditional British structure gaining popularity in glamping
- Unique historical appeal
- Distinct from cabins and other structures
- Mentioned in codebase analysis

**Definition:** A traditional British mobile structure originally used by shepherds, featuring a curved roof, wooden construction, and compact interior, now adapted for glamping with modern amenities while maintaining rustic charm.

**SEO Keywords:** shepherd's hut, shepherd hut glamping, traditional shepherd hut, British glamping accommodation

**Related Terms:** glamping, cabin, vintage-trailer, covered-wagon

---

#### 3. **Bubble Tent** ⭐⭐
**Rationale:**
- Unique transparent accommodation type
- High visual appeal and social media presence
- Growing trend in glamping industry
- Distinct from dome structures

**Definition:** An inflatable, transparent tent structure made from clear PVC or similar material, providing 360-degree views of the surrounding environment, popular for stargazing and immersive nature experiences.

**SEO Keywords:** bubble tent, transparent tent, inflatable tent glamping, stargazing tent, bubble dome

**Related Terms:** glamping, dome, canvas-tent, stargazing

---

#### 4. **Container Home** ⭐⭐
**Rationale:**
- Sustainable accommodation trend
- Modern, industrial aesthetic appeal
- Cost-effective construction option
- Growing popularity in glamping

**Definition:** A glamping accommodation created from repurposed shipping containers, converted into comfortable living spaces with modern amenities, offering a sustainable and modern architectural aesthetic.

**SEO Keywords:** container home, shipping container glamping, container accommodation, repurposed container, industrial glamping

**Related Terms:** glamping, tiny-home, cabin, sustainable-accommodation

---

### Priority 2: Niche/Unique Terms (High Differentiation Value)

#### 5. **Wall Tent** ⭐⭐
**Rationale:**
- Distinct from safari tent (different construction)
- Traditional camping structure
- Specific market segment
- Differentiates from other canvas tent types

**Definition:** A rectangular canvas tent with vertical walls and a peaked roof, similar to safari tents but typically smaller and more traditional, often used for hunting camps and rustic glamping experiences.

**SEO Keywords:** wall tent, canvas wall tent, traditional wall tent, hunting tent glamping

**Related Terms:** canvas-tent, safari-tent, bell-tent

---

#### 6. **Lotus Belle Tent** ⭐
**Rationale:**
- Specific brand/style of bell tent
- Popular in glamping market
- Distinct features from standard bell tents
- Brand recognition value

**Definition:** A specific style of bell tent known for its larger size, higher center height, and enhanced ventilation, designed for comfortable glamping with more interior space than traditional bell tents.

**SEO Keywords:** lotus belle tent, lotus bell tent, large bell tent, luxury bell tent

**Related Terms:** bell-tent, canvas-tent, glamping

---

#### 7. **Stargazing Dome** ⭐⭐
**Rationale:**
- Specialized dome variant
- High market appeal for astronomy enthusiasts
- Premium pricing potential
- Distinct from general dome term

**Definition:** A specialized geodesic dome structure designed with transparent or retractable roof sections optimized for stargazing, often featuring premium amenities and positioned for optimal night sky viewing.

**SEO Keywords:** stargazing dome, astronomy dome, transparent dome glamping, night sky viewing accommodation

**Related Terms:** dome, glamping, treehouse

---

#### 8. **Hobbit House** ⭐⭐
**Rationale:**
- Unique earth-sheltered design
- High visual appeal and social media presence
- Distinct architectural style
- Growing niche market

**Definition:** An earth-sheltered glamping accommodation built partially underground or into a hillside, featuring curved, organic architecture inspired by J.R.R. Tolkien's hobbit dwellings, with modern amenities integrated into the natural landscape.

**SEO Keywords:** hobbit house, earth-sheltered accommodation, underground glamping, hobbit hole glamping

**Related Terms:** glamping, cabin, dome, unique-accommodation

---

### Priority 4: Specialized/Regional Terms

#### 12. **Floating Cabin** ⭐
**Rationale:**
- Water-based accommodation
- Unique market segment
- High appeal for lake/river settings
- Distinct from regular cabins

**Definition:** A cabin structure built on a floating platform or pontoon system, positioned on lakes, rivers, or other bodies of water, providing a unique water-based glamping experience with modern amenities.

**SEO Keywords:** floating cabin, water cabin, lake cabin glamping, floating accommodation

**Related Terms:** cabin, glamping, water-accommodation

---

#### 13. **Igloo** ⭐
**Rationale:**
- Seasonal/regional accommodation
- Unique experience
- High social media appeal
- Specialized market

**Definition:** A dome-shaped structure made from snow, ice, or modern materials designed to resemble traditional Inuit igloos, providing a unique winter glamping experience in cold climates.

**SEO Keywords:** igloo glamping, ice hotel, snow accommodation, winter glamping igloo

**Related Terms:** dome, glamping, seasonal-accommodation

---

#### 14. **Cave Dwelling** ⭐
**Rationale:**
- Unique geological accommodation
- Historical appeal
- Rare but distinctive
- High differentiation value

**Definition:** A glamping accommodation created within a natural or carved cave, offering a unique underground experience with modern amenities integrated into the cave's natural structure.

**SEO Keywords:** cave glamping, cave accommodation, underground glamping, cave dwelling

**Related Terms:** glamping, hobbit-house, unique-accommodation
