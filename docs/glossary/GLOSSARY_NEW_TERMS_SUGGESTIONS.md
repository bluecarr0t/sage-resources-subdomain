# New Glossary Terms Suggestions for Outdoor Hospitality Industry

## Overview

This document provides suggestions for new glossary terms to expand the outdoor hospitality glossary beyond the current 29 terms. Terms are organized by category and prioritized based on:
1. Terms referenced but not yet defined
2. High SEO value ("what is" queries)
3. Industry-specific terminology
4. Internal linking opportunities

## Notes

- All terms should follow the existing glossary structure with:
  - Short definition (featured snippet ready)
  - Extended definition (300-500 words)
  - Examples (2-3 real-world scenarios)
  - Use cases (2-3 applications)
  - Related terms (3-5 internal links)
  - SEO keywords (5-10 terms)
  - Internal links to landing pages/services
  - FAQ section (3-5 questions)

- Terms should be written from the perspective of outdoor hospitality industry professionals, investors, and developers.

- Each term should include practical examples relevant to glamping resorts, RV parks, and campgrounds.

- Maintain consistency with existing glossary tone and style.
